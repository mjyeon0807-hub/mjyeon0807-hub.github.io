A metagenome-assembled genome (MAG) is a genome reconstructed computationally from metagenomic sequencing reads, without needing to culture the organism.

MAG-level metagenomic analysis of cecal contents was used to pinpoint the specific gut bacterial taxa (including Erysipelotrichaceae/Dubosiella and Lachnospiraceae) responsible for exercise-induced increases in formate production.
