PICRUSt2 is a bioinformatic tool that predicts functional (metagenomic) pathway abundance from 16S rRNA amplicon sequencing data, without requiring full metagenomic sequencing.

It was used alongside metagenome-assembled genome (MAG)-level analysis to help identify which gut bacterial taxa were responsible for exercise-induced enrichment of the formate-producing *pfl* gene.
