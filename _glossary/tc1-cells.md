Tc1 cells are type-1 cytotoxic CD8 T cells, characterized by IFN-γ and TNF-α production and the transcription factors T-bet and Eomes. They are the primary antitumor effector population studied in exercise-oncology research.

The gut-microbiota metabolite **formate** drives naive CD8 T cell differentiation into Tc1 effectors via Nrf2 activation and a pSTAT1–IRF8 signaling axis, restraining melanoma growth and enhancing checkpoint inhibitor efficacy.
