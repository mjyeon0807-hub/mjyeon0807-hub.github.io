A circulating cancer stem cell (cCSC) is a subpopulation of circulating tumor cells with stem-like self-renewal and tumor-initiating capacity, typically marked by CD44, CD133, ALDH1, SOX2, OCT4, and NANOG expression.

cCSCs act as "metastatic founders" and are associated with chemoresistance and poor prognosis.
