Vasculogenic mimicry (VM) is the formation of perfusable, vessel-like networks by tumor cells themselves, without true transdifferentiation into endothelial cells.

It reflects endothelial-like plasticity, marked by VE-cadherin (CD144) expression, driven by TGF-β, hypoxia, and SNAIL signaling.
