## Welcome

Welcome to the **Lab of Molecular and Cellular Crosstalk (MXC)** at **Chungbuk National University, College of Medicine**.

We investigate how molecular and cellular communication shapes disease biology, with a focus on cancer and immunity.

---

## Research keywords

- **Tumor microenvironment**
- **Extracellular vesicles**
- **Cancer immunology**
- **Mitochondria fitness**

---

## Research overview

- Intercellular communication and signaling in the tumor microenvironment  
- Extracellular vesicles as mediators of immune regulation and cancer progression  
- Cancer immunology and mechanisms of anti-tumor immunity  
- Mitochondrial fitness and metabolic adaptation in immune and cancer cells
